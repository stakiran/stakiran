# About Me [Japanese](onepager.md) | [English](onepager_EN.md)
stakiran (Suta Kirano)

![](assets/img/260429_110131.png)

While working as an IT engineer at a large enterprise, I explore work methods, intellectual production, and ways of collaborating. Drawing on my experience as a neurodivergent person, I have observed individual adaptation strategies and friction within organizations, and have continued to verbalize, structure, conceptualize, and turn them into tools. I have also published the results as articles, books, software, and documentation. I am currently aiming to connect insights developed at the individual level to organizational validation and social implementation.

## Areas of Interest
- Problem definition over problem solving: How can practices such as verbalization, structuring, and conceptualization gain legitimacy?
- Collaboration that works in a VUCA and DEIB era: What does collaboration look like when it takes developmental traits, cognitive traits, and lifestyle orientations into account?
- Establishing self-directed exploration: How can we build systems for intellectual production, task management, and self-management?
- Implementing diversity: How can we move diversity beyond ideals and embed it into operations?
